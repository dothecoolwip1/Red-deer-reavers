About Us

The Red Deer Reavers are Alberta's newest Historical Armored Combat Sports team. As part of HACSA, we compete in full-contact medieval combat, donning historically accurate armor and wielding blunt steel weapons in battles that test both strength and strategy. Our team is growing and welcomes newcomers to join in the fun, whether for casual participation or competitive fighting.

Christmas Armored Combat Tournament

Join us this Christmas for an epic armored combat tournament featuring teams from Alberta and Western Canada. The winner will receive a custom prize crafted by local blacksmith, MOUNT CROM Armouries Inc.

Event Highlights

  • Indoor tournament with full armor combat
  • Prize from MOUNT CROM Armouries
  • Soft kit practice for newcomers
  • Try on real armor
  • BBQ with hot dogs and hamburgers

Fun for the Whole Family

This event isn't just for armored combat enthusiasts—it's fun for the whole family! Children of all ages can enjoy face painting, soft kit practice sessions, and the opportunity to take photos with "Sir Santa" — a medieval Santa knight! Schools are encouraged to attend as part of their field trips for a chance to experience medieval history firsthand.
